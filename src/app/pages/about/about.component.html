About Us

Inspired from the learning and ideology of Shri Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Paramedical Institute (SVPPI) was founded with the egalitarian goal of ensuring equitable access to quality education to all.It has become a trusted name in the field of paramedical health sciences education.

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Paramedical Institute (SVPPI) is registered in U.P Administration Govt. of India under the Act 1882. Our Institute is an ISO 9001:2015 certified for its quality training. SVPPI is working for promotion of the Paramedical Health Education and committed to create world class professional in the field of Paramedical Science.

SVPPI is managed and run by group of highly motivated & revered healthcare leaders with zeal for transforming the field of paramedical & allied healthcare landscape. We are affiliated with renowned forerunners of healthcare world- hospital groups and chain of healthcare clinics to enable our students experience, learn, acclimatize and internalize the best in class clinical practices from the leaders.
